The Mentor Program

Those who have are need help learning new strategies or are struggling in wars will have a mentor to help them get better. This mainly applies to new members who are not yet familiar with our attack strategies. Anyone that wants one will recieve a mentor, and are expected to let the mentors help them with their war attacks.

The mentors are selected by leadership (elders and above), to qualify for the role of mentor, one must be willing to help out "mentees", have at least a 2.5 average at Town Hall 8, at least a 2.5 average at Town Hall 9, and at least a 2.1 average at TH 10. The mentors are expected to be helpful to their mentees and are expected to plan the mentee's attack with him/her.

The program ends for the mentee when he has "graduated," or reached an acceptable war average, which is a 2.4 or above average at Town Hall 8, a 2.3 or above average at Town Hall 9, and a 2.0 or above average at Town Hall 10.
