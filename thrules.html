Specific Rules for TH9s

  • Learn 3-Star strategies. If you don't know what these are ask us and we'll teach you. DO NOT GOWIPE.
  • Do not upgrade to TH10 without talking to us first. Because we're a competitive war clan, we'd strongly suggest that you do TH9.5 until your TH10 troops and spells are maxed. Watch this YouTube video.

Town Hall 8.5 Upgrade Philosophy

Once you upgrade your Town Hall to 9, don't upgrade any defenses until you can comfortably 3 star a mid TH9 base in war. The reasoning is simple: Pull your weight in war. If your base gets matched with a TH9 base you can't 3 star because your heroes, troops, and spells are underleveled then you do us a disservice.

Immediate upgrades:
Spell Factory, Laboratory, CC, AQ

Next upgrades:
Camps, Dark Barracks, and DE drills for elixir, traps for gold

Other pointers:

  • Prioritize troop upgrades (witches 2, hogs 5, golems 4, hounds 2, minions 5) over hero upgrades
  • Prioritize war troop upgrades over farming upgrades.
  • Walls, mines, and collectors when there's free builders and resources.
  • Don't upgrade valks until your AQ and BK are 30.
