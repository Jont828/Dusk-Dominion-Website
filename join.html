Requirements

*We are currently recruiting TH9s and TH10s*

  • Town Hall 9: 30+ total hero levels and be familiar with at least one 3 star army.
  • Town Hall 10: 50+ total hero levels and be able to consistently 2 star.
  • Have 300 or more war stars.
  • Come prepared to learn.
  • Be mature (regardless of your age), active, and friendly.

How Do I Join?

Apply to Dusk Dominion (#2YYVCU8C) in game. Be sure to mention that you heard about us from the clan website. We also use GroupMe to coordinate wars and to chat as outside of the game, so be prepared to create an account there.
