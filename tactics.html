TH8: Base Design

  • One double giant bomb (DGB). This DGB need to have the following characteristics:
    • Should look like a hamburger: double gb is the meat and the buns are defenses.
    • There should also not be any other defenses to the side of the DGB so the hogs won't be confused. The hog should hit one defense, then through the bombs into the other defense.
  • Centralized, not-easily pullable CC. There should be defenses all around the radius of the CC, or a tesla to trap pull troops.

TH9 & TH8.5: Base Designs

Good TH9 war bases share the following characteristics:

  • Anti-hog: Two sets of double giant bombs (DGBs). These DGB need to have the following characteristics:
    • Should look like a hamburger: double gb is the meat and the buns are defenses.
    • There should also not be any other defenses to the side of the DGB so the hogs won't be confused. The hog should hit one defense, then through the bombs into the other defense.
    • Don't put DGB AQ side. It'll just get blown up by gowiwi or gohero.
    • Multiple fake DGB spots. If you only have blank spaces for your DGB, you tell everyone where your bombs are. Having multiple (at least 2) fake DGB spots will make your base infinitely harder to attack.
  • Centralized, not-easily pullable CC. There should be defenses all around the radius of the CC, or a tesla to trap pull troops.
  • Centralized AQ.
  • Anti-air:
    • Each Wizard Tower (WT) radius can't be touching any Air Defense (AD). If it is then it means that the WT is trying to knock out the hound attacking the AD. This is bad. You want the WT to be attacking loons.
    • Pair two black air mines wiTHan AD and another pair to the opposite side AD. This will make the hounds blow up once they get close enough to the AD. Place these mines on AD that are away from the AQ so when you get golava'ed AQ-side the air mines are still effective.
    • Stick an orange air bomb next to each WT.
